The Face Behind the Spoon

Hi! I'm Jenny and I'm glad you're here! I am a writer, photographer, and recipe developer at this here blog. In the kitchen, I'm into creating colorful, healthy, flexible recipes to suit a variety of dietary needs. Spoon With Me is about creating foods you love, that also love you back. I believe that healthy eating isn't a drag when you get creative and have a little fun with it.
